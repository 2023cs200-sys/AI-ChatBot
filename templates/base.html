Our story

Coffee, brewed with intention

Hearth & Cup started as a single roaster in a converted garage, chasing the idea that coffee tastes better when there's nowhere you need to be. That feeling is still the whole point.

Every cup is poured by hand, every bean is roasted in small batches, and every table is set so a five-minute stop can turn into an hour without anyone minding.
